About Hotels Sights 24 Hour Guide

Seattle is a major city in the Northwest. Located in Washington state, the city is known for its landmark Space Needle, large ship port, music scene, tech companies, and sports teams such as the Seattle Seahawks and the Seattle Mariners. Nestled between two mountain ranges and situated on the Puget Sound, Seattle offers visitors both exciting sightseeing adventures and scenic landscapes to relax and unwind.

Seattle Center

Seattle Center is a 74-acre campus of cultural, educational, and family attractions, including the famous Seattle Space Needle, the Museum of Pop Culture, Chihuly Garden and Glass, the Climate Pledge Arena, the Pacific Science Center, and so much more. Visit the Seattle Center website to learn more about its expansive offerings or explore the center through the campus map.

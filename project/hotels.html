Hotels

Whether you are on a budget or looking for luxury, there are many places to stay during your visit to Seattle. Explore four of our favorites below or see more options at Visit Seattle.

The Edgewater Hotel

Rated the #2 hotel in Seattle by Travel + Leisure, the iconic Edgewater Hotel is located on Seattle’s waterfront and offers guests spectacular views of the Puget Sound and Olympic Mountains. Built in 1962 for the World’s Fair, the Edgewater Hotel has hosted many legendary musicians including The Beatles, Elvis Presley, The Rolling Stones, and David Bowie. To honor their musical heritage, the hotel includes a complimentary 2 hour guitar rental and access to vinyl records and record players as part of your stay.

Hyatt House Seattle/Downtown

Located across the street from Seattle Center and its landmark Space Needle, Hyatt House provides quick and easy access to many of Seattle’s popular tourist attractions including the Museum of Pop Culture, Pacific Science Center, the Seattle Center Armory Food & Event Hall, and much more. The Hyatt House is an extended stay hotel offering pet-friendly rooms with full kitchens and access to a rooftop patio with lounge seating and fire pits.

CitizenM Seattle Southlake Union Hotel

Located near Amazon headquarters and still only a 15 minute walk to the Space Needle, CitizenM is a designer hotel without the designer price. While each room includes a super-sized XL king bed next to a picture window overlooking the city, the real beauty of the hotel lies in its artistic façade, lobby and meeting rooms. In addition to its aesthetic appeal, the hotel is eco-conscious and certified as a LEED Gold facility.

Homewood Suites by Hilton Seattle Downtown

Just a seven-minute walk to the Climate Pledge Arena, the Homewood Suites is an ideal location if your stay in Seattle includes tickets to see the NHL Krakens or the WNBA Storm. Each pet-friendly extended stay suite includes a full kitchen and separate living room, plus the Queen Anne Beerhall is located just next door for after game celebrations.
